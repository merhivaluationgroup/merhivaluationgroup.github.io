Damodaran: A Thought Leader

Written by: Oday Merhi

​Aswath Damodaran is one of the most reputable names in the world of finance. In the realm of valuation, he is one of the most referenced professors when it comes to educating the young minds of tomorrow. After achieving his PhD in finance, Professor Damodaran advanced towards a fruitful career with NYU’s Stern School of Business (approximately 30 years). He is a successful author, where titles from “The Little Book of Valuation” to “Narrative and Numbers” highlight his way of thinking and deep insight. Professor Damodaran analysis both the story (history and future) and the numbers of a given company. Through the story, we can understand the numbers and by understanding the numbers’ previous path we then become more confident in understanding their future route. Damodaran’s perspective and books are shared throughout the universities of world, where it seems fit for us to refer to his leading thoughts within our future posts. If we have a perspective on a certain topic, we will then research to see if one of the leading minds (Prof. Damodaran) in finance agrees or disagrees with our perspective.
